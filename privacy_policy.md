# Privacy Policy for Aioli Recipe Manager

**Effective Date:** January 10, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 10, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Aioli Recipe Manager ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our recipe management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us

#### Account Information
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Password (encrypted and securely stored)
- Display name or username (optional)

#### Recipe Data
- Recipes you create, import, or remix
- Recipe titles, ingredients, directions, and notes
- Photos you upload or associate with recipes
- Recipe categories and favorites
- Shopping lists you create

#### Usage Information
- Features you use within the app
- Recipes you view, edit, or share
- AI remix requests and preferences
- Import history (web URLs, number of imports)

### Information Collected Automatically

#### Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Device identifiers (for crash reporting and analytics)

#### Log Data
- App usage statistics
- Error logs and crash reports
- Performance metrics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### Core Functionality
- Provide and maintain recipe management features
- Sync your data across devices (if signed in)
- Process AI recipe remixing requests
- Import recipes from external websites
- Generate shopping lists from recipes

### Improvement and Development
- Analyze app usage to improve features
- Debug errors and fix bugs
- Develop new features based on usage patterns
- Optimize app performance

### Communication
- Send important service updates
- Respond to your support requests
- Notify you about subscription status (if applicable)

### Legal and Security
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and the rights of others

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Your recipes are primarily stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- Local data is protected by your device's security features
- Data persists even when offline

### Cloud Sync (Optional)
- If you create an account, your data is securely synced to our servers
- We use Supabase for cloud storage and authentication
- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
- Data at rest is encrypted in our cloud database

### Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Secure authentication using industry standards
- Regular security audits and updates
- Limited access to personal data by staff
